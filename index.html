About XD Magazine

XD Magazine celebrates Filipino narratives, focusing on stories that highlight challenges and successes. The name "XD" evokes nostalgia and joy, symbolizing the shared experiences and memories of Filipinos.

Our mission is to entertain, inform, and inspire by delivering high-quality, engaging content that reflects the unique Filipino identity.
